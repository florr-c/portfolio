Project Overview

BookPoint is an online bookstore offering users the opportunity to browse and purchase books, provide reviews to authors, and conveniently acquire gift cards for unique gifting experiences.

Project Goals

The project goal for BookPoint is to establish a seamless and user-centric online platform that facilitates browsing, purchasing, and reviewing books while also providing a convenient avenue for customers to purchase gift cards, thereby enhancing the overall shopping experience and fostering meaningful engagement between readers and authors.
