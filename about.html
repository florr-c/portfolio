Hi Everyone!
I am Florencia Castelvero

I am an experienced animator, UX/UI designer and web developer deeply committed to crafting visually captivating designs that prioritize user experience. With degrees in design, animation, and web programming, I have collaborated with diverse clients and prestigious brands, refining my skills and staying updated on emerging technologies. I have excelled in various roles within advertising agencies, television networks, and multinational corporations, contributing to notable projects for renowned brands such as Disney Channel Latin America, Fox Sports, Samsung, Despegar.com, and Mercadolibre.

During my seven-year experience at IBM, I thrived as a UX/UI designer, brand strategist, and web developer, delivering innovative solutions for the company. Additionally, I contributed to developing and implementing web-based automation solutions within IBM, aiding clients in streamlining processes and optimizing efficiency by reducing hours spent on non-essential tasks. Motivated by a dedication to excellence, I continuously expand my expertise and using the latest tools to offer the best service possible. My design philosophy is rooted in meticulous attention to detail, proactive problem-solving, and a dedication to driving innovation.
